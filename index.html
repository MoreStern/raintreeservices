Services

We perform a wide range of roofing, sheet metal, coating and restoration services

Roof Replacement and Recover

Offering Built-Up, Modified Bitumen, Metal, EPDM, TPO, PVC, Roof Coating and other options.

New Construction Roofing

Installing various new roof systems in our communities.

Fall Protection

Skylight safety cages, perimeter fall protection and warning lines, roof ladder cages and more.

Roof Leak Investigation

Chasing down day-to-day roof leaks that inconvenience our customers.

Scope Roof Repairs

Perform tenant fit-out roof repairs, new roof curb installations, snow rail and snow guard repairs, etc.

Preventative Maintenance

Annual and bi-annual preventative maintenance programs available to prolong roof life cycles.

Roof Inspections

Perform visual and destructive testing to troubleshoot problematic areas.

Roof Asset Management Reports

Generate detailed roof reports including current conditions, recommendations and budgets.

Disaster Response

24/7/365 service to our customers when they need us most.

Pre-Storm and Post-Storm Inspections

Preparing for the impact and inspecting the aftermath of severe storms.

Roof Top Snow Removal

Performing weights & measures, clearing at drainage locations, “windrowing,” and 100% removal.

Painting, Sealant and Deck Coating

Offering light exterior building envelope restoration services.

Masonry Restoration

Damproofing, waterproofing, and minor masonry repairs.

Roof Coating

Prolonging roofs' life expectancies with a wide range of coating systems.

About

We are a highly experienced group with a focus on quality, open communication and professionalism.

We are a commercial roofing and sheet metal contractor located in York County, PA with satellite crews operating out of Southern Maryland and Pennsylvania's Lehigh Valley. Established by Marlin Potteiger (past president of the National Roofing Contractors Association), Thomas Potteiger, and Scott Ierley, we have over 150 years of combined experience serving the Mid Atlantic region.


Our goal and moto is to "create long lasting, profitable relationships through superior service and quality while never compromising our core business and family values".
